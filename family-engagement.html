Parental & Community Engagement

Building stronger communities through meaningful partnerships between families, schools, and community organizations. Our engagement program recognizes that student success is a collaborative effort involving the entire community.

Parent Education & Support Programs

Empowering parents with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to support their children's academic, emotional, and social development.

Parenting Skills Workshops:

  • Adolescent Development: Understanding teenage brain development and behavior
  • Communication Strategies: Effective parent-child communication techniques
  • Homework Support: How to help without doing the work
  • Discipline & Boundaries: Positive parenting approaches
  • Academic Motivation: Encouraging school success without pressure

Specialized Parent Support:

  • Single-parent family challenges and solutions
  • Blended family dynamics and adjustment
  • Parents of students with learning differences
  • High-achieving student parent support
  • Parents of struggling students

Parent Resource Library:

  • Books and materials on child development
  • Jamaica Education System information
  • Scholarship and financial aid resources
  • Mental health and wellness materials
  • Career planning guides for families

School-Family Partnership Development

Creating meaningful collaborations between families and educational institutions to ensure every student receives comprehensive support.

School Partnership Initiatives:

  • Parent-Teacher Collaboration: Regular communication and goal-setting meetings
  • Family Engagement in Learning: Home-based learning activities and projects
  • Parent Volunteer Programs: Opportunities for families to contribute to school community
  • Family Academic Planning: Long-term educational goal setting with families
  • Cultural Celebration Events: Celebrating diversity and inclusion

Communication Systems:

  • Regular parent-student-counselor meetings
  • Family progress reports and success celebrations
  • Multilingual communication for diverse families
  • Digital communication platforms and portals
  • Parent feedback and suggestion systems

Family-Friendly School Events:

  • Family academic achievement celebrations
  • Parent education series and workshops
  • Family career day and opportunity fairs
  • Cultural and community building events
  • Student-led parent conferences

Special Needs Support:

  • Transition planning for post-secondary success

Educational Equity Initiatives:
