Meet Our Counselor

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Ph.D. School Psychology, Licensed Professional Counselor

Dr. Johnson brings over 15 years of experience in educational counseling and student development, with a particular focus on Jamaican youth and Caribbean educational systems. She specializes in adolescent psychology, academic motivation, college readiness, and cultural identity development. Her warm, approachable style creates a safe space where students from all backgrounds feel comfortable discussing personal challenges, academic pressures, and future aspirations.

Throughout her career, Dr. Johnson has worked extensively with students preparing for CSEC and CAPE examinations, provided career guidance for university applications, and supported students navigating the transition from secondary to tertiary education. She has published research on culturally competent counseling practices and regularly presents at regional education conferences.

Credentials and Professional Affiliations

  • Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) - Jamaica
  • National Certified Counselor (NCC) - NBCC
  • Ph.D. in School Psychology - University of the West Indies
  • Certified Career Counselor (CCC) - NBCC
  • Member, Jamaica Psychological Association
  • Certified Trauma-Informed Care Practitioner

Our Impact & History

2018

Hub Founded

Established with a vision to provide comprehensive student support services, recognizing the unique needs of Jamaican youth in academic, personal, and career development. Initial focus on academic counseling and study skills support.

2020

Expanded Services

Added comprehensive career exploration and college readiness programs, including CSEC/CAPE preparation, university application guidance, and career assessment tools tailored for Jamaican students.

2022

Community Recognition

Awarded "Excellence in Student Support" by the Jamaica Education Association for outstanding contributions to student development and innovative counseling approaches that integrate cultural competence.

2024

500+ Students Served

Reached milestone of helping over 500 students achieve their academic and personal goals. Expanded services to include family engagement programs, community partnerships, and digital counseling resources.
